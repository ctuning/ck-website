Get in touch with the CK community

  • The international cTuning foundation (non-profit R&D organization) and dividiti lead the international consortium to coordinate development, usage and standardization of the open-source Collective Knowledge technology (CK) and reusable AI/ML components. We help researchers to adopt CK while automating and unifying tedious benchmarking, optimization and co-design of the efficient software/hardware stack for emerging workloads (AI, ML, quantum) in terms of speed, accuracy, energy, size, costs and other metrics across diverse models, data sets, libraries, frameworks and platforms using portable, customizable and open-source CK workflows.

    Contact cTuning foundation and dividiti if you are interested to join the CK consortium or you need our help to automate your experiments, minimize R&D costs, accelerate knowledge discovery particularly for AI and ML, and facilitate technology transfer!

  • Join technical and research discussions using Collective Knowledge or cTuning mailing lists.
  • Join Artifact Evaluation google group to participate in discussions on reproducible research at systems and ML conferences.
  • Join our LinkedIn group on collaborative and reproducible systems/ML/AI research.
  • Join CK slack channel or catch us on IRC channel #ctuning @ freenode.net (you may need to ping us in advance since we moved to Slack).
  • Follow CK-related news on non-profit cTuning foundation twitter, cTuning blog, cTuning facebook, dividiti twitter and dividiti blog.
